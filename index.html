Your body changes every week. Your training should too.

Most running programs treat every week the same. But your energy, strength, and recovery shift with your menstrual cycle. HerRhythm is the first running program in India designed around these natural rhythms — so you train smarter, feel stronger, and actually enjoy the process.

The Science
Four phases. Four training strategies.
Each phase of your cycle brings different strengths. We design your training to match — not fight — what your body is doing.

Menstrual

Days 1 – 5

Your body is recovering. We honour that with gentle movement, shorter distances, and recovery-focused sessions. No guilt, no pressure.

Recovery & rest

Follicular

Days 6 – 13

Energy rises. Estrogen climbs. This is when we build — increasing volume, adding strength work, and preparing for your peak.

Build & grow

Ovulatory

Days 14 – 16

Your power week. Strength, speed, and endurance peak. We push tempo runs, intervals, and longer distances here.

Peak performance

Luteal

Days 17 – 28

Progesterone rises. We taper effort, maintain consistency, and focus on moderate steady-state work as your body prepares to reset.

Maintain & taper
